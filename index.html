The name Silvertown carries a story unlike any other ‚Äî a district in East London's Royal Docks that once connected the world through the first transatlantic telegraph cables.

Like Silvertown, Mombasa is a historic port city built on trade, connection, and resilience. One vision unites them: a world-class school that connects the local to the global.
